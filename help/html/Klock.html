    Klock - A clock with a K


    A multifunction timing thingy, where some [but not necessary all] of the functions are useful.


    The multifunction timing thingy is split into many Thingy-ma-bobs - Fuzzy Time, Countdown, Timer, Event, Reminder & Clipboard Monitor.
